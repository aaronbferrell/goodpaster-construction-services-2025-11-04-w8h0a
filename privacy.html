Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and browsing behavior. Cookies can be either session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored on your device for a set period).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Track website analytics and user behavior
  • Support security and fraud prevention measures
  • Deliver targeted advertising content

Types of Cookies We Use

Our website may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly and provide services you request.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website and identify areas for improvement.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices and preferences to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertising and measure campaign effectiveness.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our website. For more information about managing cookies, please consult your browser's help documentation.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Compliance with industry security standards

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You acknowledge that any information you transmit is at your own risk.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors and contractors who assist us in operating our website and providing services, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request to disclose your information.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Your Consent: With your explicit permission or at your request to share information with third parties.
  • Aggregate Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you personally for research and marketing purposes.
